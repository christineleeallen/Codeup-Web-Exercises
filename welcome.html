about me...

My career began with a teaching degree from Western Michigan University. I moved to Texas in 2005, and taught middle and high school level classes in San Antonio. Despite my love of teaching, I always harbored a secret love for web development and design. In 2016, I made the leap into technology by attending Codeup, a web development school located in the technology hub of downtown San Antonio, Texas. Now I'm living my dream of developing and styling web pages!
