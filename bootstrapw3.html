My journey into technology...

My career began with a teaching degree from Western Michigan University. After moving to Texas in 2005, I taught middle and high school classes in San Antonio. Despite my love of teaching, I always harbored a secret love for web development and design. In 2016, I leaped head first into this new world by attending Codeup, a full stack coding school located in the bustling technology hub of downtown San Antonio, Texas. Today, I'm living my dream of developing and styling web pages for clients just like you!
